Shadow Call – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shadow Call (“the App”) handles information when you use our fake call and call simulation features. Shadow Call is published by Apllex (“we”, “our”, or “us”) and is designed to simulate realistic phone calls for privacy, social comfort, and planned interruption scenarios. It does not provide real telephony or VoIP services.

By installing or using Shadow Call, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview – Key Points

  • No account required: No account or sign-in needed.
  • Data stored locally: Caller profiles, schedules, and settings are stored on your device (SharedPreferences).
  • No real call routing: Uses Android Telecom APIs for simulation only.
  • Limited personal data use: Only to provide fake call and dialer simulation.
  • Third-party services: Google AdMob (ads) and Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) may collect and process data under their own policies.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Information You Provide

  • Caller profiles and presets: Data: fake caller name, number, optional photo/label. Use: generate realistic fake calls. Stored locally.
  • Scheduled call details: Data: caller profile, schedule time, repeat mode, alarm IDs. Use: trigger fake calls. Stored locally and in Android alarm storage.
  • App settings and preferences: Data: vibration, auto-answer/hangup, ringtone, SIM labels. Use: control behavior and display. Stored locally.
  • Custom audio / text-to-speech: Data: audio recordings, TTS text. Use: playback during fake calls. Stored locally; not uploaded.

Data is processed only to provide App features and is never sold.

Information from Device Permissions

  • Phone & call-related (READ_PHONE_STATE, MANAGE_OWN_CALLS): register/manage fake call account, display system call UI, manage call lifecycle.
  • Call log (READ_CALL_LOG, WRITE_CALL_LOG): optionally insert fake calls for realistic call history.
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): suggest contacts for fake caller profiles.
  • Exact alarm (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): schedule fake calls and restore alarms after reboot.
  • Foreground service / notifications (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, POST_NOTIFICATIONS): run background services and show call notifications.

Permissions can be granted or revoked anytime via Android settings.

Automatically Collected Information

  • On-device technical data: Logs of schedules, errors, call states. Stored locally for debugging/reliability.
  • Third-party analytics / ads (Google AdMob): device identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions. Processed per third-party privacy policies.

No backend server tracks usage across devices.

3. In-App Purchases

  • Uses Google Play Billing for ad removal or premium features.
  • Google may process purchase tokens, order details, basic Google account info.
  • We receive only purchase success/failure info. Payment details are not shared.

See Google Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

  • Google AdMob: Ads, may use device identifiers.
  • Google Play Services / Billing: App distribution, crash reporting, secure purchases.

Third parties are independent controllers. We do not sell personal data. Disclosure only as required by law or to protect rights.

5. Data Storage, Retention, and Security

  • Local storage: caller profiles, schedules, settings, logs.
  • Retention: only as needed for app functionality or legal obligations.
  • Security: reasonable measures; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Your Choices and Controls

  • Permissions: grant/revoke via Android settings; revoking may reduce features.
  • Local data management: delete presets/schedules or uninstall app.
  • Ads: may opt out of personalized ads if options are available.
